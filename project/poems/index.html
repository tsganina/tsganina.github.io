Atlanteans will drop the sky

In this collection you will find poems. Different - sad and funny, serious and joking, about magic and “about life”. About love, about hatred and betrayal, about hopelessness and belief in a miracle.

Life is a game? So it went to the edge!

Blaze, perish and create.

Remember: we burn our souls,

To be calm inside.

Northern Horizon

Poems written in 2022. For me, this year has become quite difficult, marked the transition to a completely different life.

August smells of honey and hops

Somewhere in the sky, the moon is silvering

Hiding under a spreading spruce

And I’ll wait until the lightning flashes

The second book is illustrated using AI

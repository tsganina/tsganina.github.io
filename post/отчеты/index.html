Reports

##What is a report?

A report is a document containing information about the work performed, the result of activities, events that have occurred or facts. It can be designed in various formats and applied in various fields, such as accounting, project management, medicine, scientific research, etc. The main purpose of the report is to convey information and feedback.

##Why do I need a report?

The report is needed to document and analyze information about activities or processes. It allows you to evaluate performance, determine success and problems in work, and make decisions based on the data obtained. Reports play an important role in organization, business, research, project management, etc. Depending on the goals and objectives, reports may have a different format and structure, but they are all aimed at providing information for decision-making.

##Report content

  1. The title page with the number of the laboratory work and the full name of the student.
  2. Formulation of the purpose of the work.
  3. Description of the results of the task: – screenshots (screenshots) that record the performance of laboratory work; – listings (source code) of programs (if any); – program execution results (text or screenshot depending on the task).
  4. Conclusions agreed with the purpose of the work.
  5. Answers to security questions.

##Our reports

Our reports are executed in the Markdown system and necessarily contain screenshots of the work. Thanks to the markup language, their design becomes an easy task.

On Monday, I wake up hard after the weekend, but I know that new tasks and things are waiting for me. Tuesday is already easier, I’m starting to get into a working rhythm. On Wednesday I usually feel excited, because it’s already the middle of the week, and I realize that there is still so much work left. On Thursday, I am already determined to win and ready to solve the most difficult tasks. Friday is a real holiday. I know that the long-awaited weekend will begin soon, and I can finally relax. I usually do my lessons on Saturday. Sunday is a day for family and loved ones, and I try to spend it with maximum benefit and enjoyment. This is how my days of the week pass.
